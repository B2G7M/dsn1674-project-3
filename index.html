About Us

Who we are

Chez Lucien is a local bar and restaurant situated in the heart of Ottawa’s downtown on Murray and Dalhousie street. We are small, cozy, and always perfect for anyone who wishes to have a great meal. With delicious burgers, and amazing salads, you can’t go wrong.
